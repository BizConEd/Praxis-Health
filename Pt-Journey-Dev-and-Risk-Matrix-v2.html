Praxis Health – Patient Journey Development & Risk Matrix

Created by Suleiry matos

Journey Step Canvas Capability Today Needs Dev Care/Risk Impact Dev Notes
Initial Contact & Appointment Scheduling Portal scheduling exists but is disabled; lacks scheduling rule enforcement. ✓ Increased scheduling errors; reduced access convenience for established patients. Enhance scheduling engine to enforce provider templates and rules. Needs Development to work
Pre-Registration & Forms capability exists but is tied to Portal, which is turned off. ✓ doesn't show document that was signed in totality, less capability to track release of information consent without external process report audit, delays in care start; risk of data entry errors from manual transcription. Digital pre-registration and consent forms with direct EHR integration.
Check-In & Status Updates Basic banner for status change exists. ✓ Patient frustration, reduced transparency, increased calls to front desk. Currently what the person at the front end does will be attached to the visit encounter note which can lend it self to many issues it would need dev work to seperate this. also does not have enough data points to provide accurate timeline of patient journey IE how long is patient
Clinical Encounter & Orders Order entry exists; referral workflow delays note sign-off until marked 'sent'. ✓ note signature impediment also delays accurate medical records/compliance for both external release and patient view in portal and Delays in billing and care coordination; potential for missed follow-up actions. Decouple referral send from note sign-off; enhance order tracking filters.
Prior Authorizations & Follow-Up Scheduling No integrated prior auth; manual tracking. ✓ Treatment delays; patient dissatisfaction from prolonged approval times. Prior auth management module; link to scheduling and patient notifications.
Payment Collection Not integrated with RCM (Candid) yet. ✓ Surprise billing; delayed collections impacting revenue cycle health. Complete Candid integration for real-time payment posting.
Billing & Claims Claims workflow tied to note completion; partial workaround with labels. ✓ Delayed reimbursement; compliance risks from incorrect coding. Fix queue automation rules (if/then logic); standardize visit type coding; enforce protocols for billable vs non-billable; clear definitions and governance for queue usage.
Billing Workflow Governance No built-in enforcement of standardized workflows or documentation requirements. ✓ Audit risk; inaccurate coding; operational inefficiency. Implement governance policies with system-enforced protocols; lock down who can modify claim-related fields; require chief complaint in patient's own words.
