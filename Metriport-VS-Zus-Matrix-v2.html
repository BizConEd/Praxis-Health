Summary & Recommendation

Current Praxis Staff Frustrations with Metriport:

  • Data Delays: Manual refresh processes causing outdated patient information during visits
  • Medication Reconciliation: Time-consuming manual reconciliation creating workflow bottlenecks
  • HEDIS Reporting: Labor-intensive manual reporting affecting quality scores
  • Canvas Integration: Generic API requiring extensive custom development resources
  • Care Gap Identification: Reactive alerts missing proactive population health opportunities

Zus Capabilities Aligned with Praxis Needs:

  • Strategic Canvas Partnership: Direct integration via Contributor Program eliminates custom development overhead
  • Real-time Data Platform: Always-on ZAP addresses data freshness concerns
  • AI-Powered Insights: Generated Patient Summary reduces provider cognitive load
  • HEDIS Optimization: Automated transitions of care tracking through Data Mart improves quality workflows
  • Revenue Enhancement: AI-driven HCC capture increases risk adjustment opportunities

Recommended Next Step:

Initiate a 90-day pilot program with Zus Health at 1-2 Canvas clinic sites to validate:

  • Canvas Contributor Program integration effectiveness
  • Provider workflow improvements
  • Data quality and timeliness gains
  • HEDIS reporting automation benefits
  • ROI measurement vs. current Metriport costs

Timeline: Q4 2025 pilot launch with full evaluation by Q1 2026 for enterprise decision.
